How to Play

W A S D  Move

🖱️  Mouse  Look around

E  Interact — extinguish candles, open doors, enter/exit hiding spots

M  Toggle map    P / Esc  Pause

Three candles keep the entity at bay. Extinguish them all to unlock the exit door, then descend to the next floor.

The entity patrols the manor. It will investigate extinguished candles and chase you on sight. Candle light repels it — stay near the flame.

Hide inside cabinets to break line of sight. If it sees you enter, it will not be fooled.

Each floor grows larger and darker. Survive as long as you can.
