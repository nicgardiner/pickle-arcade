How to Play

You stand at the baseline, viewed from behind. Your hitbox is the glowing bar in front of you — it works just like a Pong paddle.

• Move your mouse left/right to slide along the baseline.
• As the ball nears, your player swings automatically. If the bar covers the ball, you connect; if not, you whiff and lose the point.
• Where the ball meets your bar sets the return angle — center sends it straight, edges send it cross-court.
• Each exchange the rally gets faster until someone misses.
• Serving: aim with the mouse, then click or press Space.

Scoring

Points → Games → Match.

Win a rally — get the ball past your opponent — to score a point. Points climb in tennis style 0 → 15 → 30 → 40. Win a game by reaching 4 points with a two-point lead — at 40–40 it’s deuce, and you must take the advantage point and then one more.

First to 4 games wins the match (no win-by-two needed for the match). Serve alternates each game. Tournament finals are best of 3 sets — win 2 sets, each set being first to 4 games.
