How to Play
⚡ Core Rules
Each turn, both players secretly choose a move — then both execute simultaneously.
You have a shared PP pool for your whole team, and individual HP per Pokémon.
Lose 3 Pokémon and you lose the battle.
⚔ Attacks
Pick an attack tier (1–3 PP), then choose High or Low direction.
Opposite directions → both attacks connect, both take damage.
Same direction → CLASH! Higher PP wins. The difference is dealt as damage.
Type advantage gives your attack +1 PP in clashes only.
🔋 Special Actions
Recharge — restore 3 PP, but any incoming attack hits for full damage.
Evade — dodge everything this turn. Has a 3-turn cooldown.
🔀 New Pokémon
When your Pokémon faints, pick a new one from the full roster (unused only).
The new Pokémon adds 5 PP to your pool. Each Pokémon has a unique trait — read it!
👥 Multiplayer Note
Both players share one keyboard. Your move selection is hidden — the other player only sees a "Locked in!" indicator, not which move you chose.

P1 keys: 1/2/3 = attack tier  |  W = High  |  S = Low  |  A = Recharge  |  D = Evade
P2 keys: 7/8/9 = attack tier  |  I = High  |  K = Low  |  J = Recharge  |  L = Evade

If you've locked in a move, press Q (P1) or U (P2) to cancel your selection.

Note: High or Low must always be confirmed — even for Recharge and Evade. The direction doesn't affect these actions, but it still counts as your confirmation.
