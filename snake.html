// HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Guide your serpent to eat food and grow as long as possible. Don't crash into walls or your own tail. The longer you get, the higher your score.

Scoring

Each piece of food is worth 10 points. Harder difficulties multiply your score. Special golden food appears occasionally for bonus points.

Tips

• Plan ahead — you can't reverse direction.
• Hug the edges to give yourself room.
• On harder modes, walls appear as obstacles.
• Pause any time to think — no penalty.

Controls

Difficulty

🌱 Seedling — small grid, slow speed, no walls.
🐢 Scout — medium grid, moderate speed.
🦎 Ranger — large grid, fast, obstacle walls.
🐍 Viper — huge grid, very fast, dense walls.
