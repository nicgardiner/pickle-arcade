RHINO PILE-UPSTACK · CLIMB · CONQUER

A hot-seat climbing brawl up a giant stone rhino. Each player sends a climber up in turn — get your highest limb above the mark the last climber left, and don't freeze in the red OUT-OF-BOUNDS zone. Last climber to raise the bar wins.

W/D arms  ·  A/S legs  ·  hold a key & aim with the mouse, release to grab  ·  ENTER to start / freeze

Online: one host vs one joiner. Host sets up the climbers (1–6) and controls the match; both screens stay in sync.
