How to play

Move with WASD to explore the dungeon. By default your cursor is a blade — sweep it through enemies to slash; faster strokes hit harder. You can only see and strike what's within your line of sight — corners and walls hide what lurks beyond.

Enemies are corrupted desktop icons: Folders drift, Recycle Bins charge, Error dialogs split, hulking Defrag brutes soak damage, Svchost daemons fire bolts from range, and Ctrl-Alt-Del wraiths dart in fast.

Find chests and press E to take the weapon inside — your current weapon drops into that same chest, so to swap back you return to its drop spot and take it again. Press TAB anytime to view your collected items. The Greatsword hits hard but slows the cursor sharply; Twin Daggers chip light damage that stacks bleed; the Siphon Staff deals low damage that ramps the longer you hold it on one enemy (and resets when it leaves); the Glitch Bow fires on click for sword-equal damage at range; and the Wand of Entrapment deals damage only to enemies your trail completely encircles — loop the cursor all the way around a target to close the ring and, the instant it's fully trapped, hit it with one massive chunk of damage. Your starting Cursor Blade is the balanced all-rounder, and it can also turn up in chests, so a dropped blade can always be reclaimed.

Each kill grants XP. Fill the bar to level up and pick one of three upgrades. Every Floor is a fresh maze with one objective, and each carries a corruption — one floor the icons are swifter, another they hit harder, another they're tougher. Deep past floor 11 they gain all three at once. Complete the objective to unlock the stairway door hidden deep in the dungeon — stand on it and press F to descend to the Cache and the next floor. The descent is endless.
