HOW TO PLAY
The Basics
The board is a 3×3 grid of smaller 3×3 boards — nine boards in one. Win three small boards in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win the game.
The Key Rule
  • The cell position you play in determines which board your opponent must play on next.
  • Play in the top-left cell → opponent must play in the top-left board.
  • Play in the center cell → opponent must play in the center board.
  • If the required board is already won or full, your opponent may play anywhere.
Board States
  • Gold glow — you must play here.
  • Soft white glow — free move, choose any of these boards.
  • Blue border — won by X.
  • Red border — won by O.
  • Faded — unavailable this turn.
Strategy Tips
  • The center board (5) is the most strategically valuable.
  • Avoid sending your opponent to the center unless you have a plan.
  • Sending your opponent to a won or full board gives them a free move — try to avoid it.
  • Think two moves ahead: your move controls their board, their move controls yours.
